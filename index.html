About me  

My name is Amanda, an entry-level software developer.I matriculated in 2013, on 2014-2015 after matric I enrolled myself with youth development programmes ran by Department of Social Development, that is where I was introduced to YearBeyond. On 2016 I was a tutor volunteer for an organization called YearBeyond which helps children in underprivileged schools with maths and English. Starting the software development journey seemed confusing for me since I did not have any background in coding but that did not stop me from adapting to change and taking challenges as my ladder to up my skills and chase my dreams. Being a CodeX student has thought me how to approach problems in different situations using algorithm thinking, how to work in an Agile environment, I have grown as a developer, team worker but mostly I have grown as an individual. On my spare time, i enjoy reading facebook diaries or listen to music. My wish is to give back to the community one day, like helping young people with the basic computer skills by doing that I am trying to close the gap of computer illiterate in South Africa, more especially in the underprivileged townships.
